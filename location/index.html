Radical Networks takes place at Chemistry Creative, a full service creative agency, media and event production company located in the Bushwick neighborhood of Brooklyn, NY. With the help of Chemistry's production team, we take one of their raw warehouse spaces and build out a beautiful gallery and speaker's stage that can accommodate up to 400 people standing or 200 seated.

Chemistry Creative is wheelchair accessible via ramp. Please email erica@radicalnetworks.org if you have any special needs.
